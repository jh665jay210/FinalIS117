Hair Types

When regarding curl patterns, there are four categories.

  • Type 1 hair is the straightest and has the most circular follicle pattern.
  • Type 2 hair is more for the wavy girls, with 2a hair being the straightest variation and 2c being the curliest variation.
  • Type 3 hair is for all the curly heads. They have a tighter hair curl. 3a hair is a nice way-curly mix, while 3c hair is the tightest curl pattern in this category.
